What we are about

Kiareys Wings & Grill goal is to bring top tier food to the natives of the carolinas. We take pride in the quality and the variety of food we often. Even though, we are a brand new resturant we feel as if we can complete with the best of them and serve some pretty fine casual dining & take-out.

We also planning to offer delivery all over the charlotte metropolitan area. Possibility, in the near future adding a food truck on the weekend to cater to a wider audience. Our Grand Opening will be on July 22, 2024 and we hope to have an amazing turn out. Our business hours will be 11am-11pm on Tuesday through Thursday, 11am-1am on Friday-Saturday, and 11am-9am on Sundays.
